    • Bicol Express

    Cooking time: 1 hour

    Recipe

    • Heat oil in a wide pot over medium heat. Cook onions and garlic, stir occassionaly until softened.
    • Add pork, stir it occasionally until lightly browned.
    • Continue to stir occasionally for about 1-2 minutes after adding shrimp paste
    • Pour coconut milk, water, and add chili peppers. Cook it for about 35 minutes until pork is tender and the liquid is reduced.
    • Add finger chilies, stirring it occasionally for 1-2 minutes.
    • After adding coconut cream, continue until it is thickened.
    • Season with salt and pepper to your liking.
    Ingredients
    • 1 tablespoon oil
    • 1 onion, peeled and sliced thinly
    • 4 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
    • 2 pounds pork belly, cut into 1-inch cubes
    • 2 tablespoons fresh shrimp paste
    • 1 can (13.5 ounces) coconut milk
    • 1 cup water
    • 14 pieces Thai chili peppers, stemmed and minced
    • 2 cups finger chilies (siling haba), sliced
    • 1 cup coconut cream
    • salt and pepper to taste
